Finding Us

St Huxley's Community College is located in a quiet corner of a peaceful town, surrounded by parks and science labs.

To find us:

  • Take the Huxley Line subway to "Utopia Gardens"
  • Exit and walk 5 minutes west past the biodome
  • Turn left at the tree of knowledge statue
