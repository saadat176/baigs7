Brave new world

It's a brave new world out there. Our children are being placed in increasingly competitive environments — during recreation and as they transition into adulthood — facing examinations, jobs, careers, and other important life decisions. Having the wrong mindset, becoming too emotional, or making poor choices can prevent them from taking their rightful place in today’s ideal society.

As concerned parents, guardians, or carers, you undoubtedly want to give your children the best possible start in life — and you’ve come to the right place.

The best start in life

At St. Huxley’s, we take pride not only in delivering top-quality education, but also in fostering the emotional and social intelligence students need to thrive in tomorrow’s world. In addition to excelling in subjects like genetics, data mining, and chemistry, we offer compulsory training in:

  • Emotional control
  • Judgement
  • Assertion
  • Focus and resolve

If you are interested, then your next steps will likely be to:

  1. Call us for more information
  2. Ask for a brochure, which includes signup form
  3. Book a visit!
