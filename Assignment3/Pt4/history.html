Our History

Founded in 2020, "My Cool Website" began as a weekend photography blog and has grown into a full community storytelling platform. What started with balloon festivals and landscape shots now includes student events, travel stories, and educational features.

Over the years, we’ve expanded our content to reflect the voice of our contributors and the spirit of our audience. Our small team has grown, but our commitment to creativity and connection has remained the same.
