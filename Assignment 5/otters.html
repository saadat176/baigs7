The trouble with Otters

Otters look like playful swimmers—and they are—but they’re also indicators of clean rivers and healthy coasts. When pollution rises, otters vanish fast.

Protecting habitat

Riparian buffers, reduced runoff, and fish-friendly waterways support otters and the countless species that share their homes.
