Featured work

Here’s a small selection of projects we’ve completed recently. Each one blends solid engineering with thoughtful design—and yes, the occasional bear.

BearFacts — Interactive Wildlife Guide

A lightweight, mobile-first web app that helps visitors learn about wildlife with accessible components, offline-friendly media, and snappy client-side filtering.

  • Built with semantic HTML and progressive enhancement.
  • Client-side search with instant results; content works without JavaScript.
  • Media optimized for low-bandwidth environments.

Status: Live • Role: Front-end & a11y

HabitatMap — Conservation Dashboard

A data visualization dashboard that tracks habitats, populations, and seasonal migration. Designed for field researchers with readable typography and strong color contrast in all lighting conditions.

  • Exportable charts for reports; CSV/GeoJSON import.
  • Keyboard-navigable controls and screen-reader annotations.
  • Works offline with background sync.

Status: In pilot • Role: UX & data plumbing

RangerNotes — Field Notes, Simplified

A no-fuss note-taking tool for rangers and volunteers. Autosaves drafts, supports quick photo attachments, and exports to open formats so your data isn’t trapped.

  • Offline-first with conflict-aware sync.
  • Tagging and filters; bulk export to Markdown/CSV.
  • Privacy-respecting by default.

Status: Beta • Role: Full-stack
