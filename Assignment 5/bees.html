The trouble with Bees

Bees are often portrayed as picnic ruiners, but they’re essential pollinators that keep farms and wild ecosystems productive. Most species are gentle and prefer flowers to people.

Why they matter

Roughly one in three bites of food depends on pollination. Habitat loss, pesticides, and disease stress bee populations, but planting native flowers and avoiding harmful chemicals helps.
